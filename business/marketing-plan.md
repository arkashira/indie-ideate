# marketing-plan.md

## Positioning
**Primary Positioning Statement:**  
"Indie-Ideate: Your AI-powered partner for generating and validating niche software tool ideas, tailored for indie hackers and creators."

**Alternative Positioning Statements:**  
1. "Transform your creative sparks into validated software ideas with Indie-Ideate's AI-driven insights."
2. "Empower your indie journey with Indie-Ideate: the AI tool that helps you discover and monetize your next big idea."
3. "Indie-Ideate: Where indie creators turn inspiration into profitable software solutions through AI validation."

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
1. **Persona 1: Alex the Indie Hacker**  
   - **Daily Job:** Building and launching MVPs for various software projects.  
   - **Budget:** $50/month for tools that enhance productivity and ideation.

2. **Persona 2: Sarah the Content Creator**  
   - **Daily Job:** Creating digital content and looking for software tools to complement her brand.  
   - **Budget:** $30/month for tools that help streamline her creative process.

3. **Persona 3: Mike the Startup Founder**  
   - **Daily Job:** Exploring new software ideas to solve specific market problems and seeking validation before development.  
   - **Budget:** $100/month for tools that provide market insights and validation.

## Channels
1. **Social Media Advertising (Facebook, Instagram)**  
   - **CAC Estimate:** $20 per acquired customer.

2. **Content Marketing (Blogs, SEO)**  
   - **CAC Estimate:** $15 per acquired customer through organic traffic and lead generation.

3. **Partnerships with Indie Hacker Communities (e.g., Indie Hackers, Product Hunt)**  
   - **CAC Estimate:** $25 per acquired customer through community engagement and referrals.

## Content Cadence
- **Week 1:** Launch blog post introducing Indie-Ideate and its features.
- **Week 2:** Release a case study on successful ideation using Indie-Ideate.
- **Week 3:** Publish a video tutorial on how to use Indie-Ideate effectively.
- **Week 4:** Host a live Q&A session with the product team.
- **Week 5:** Share user testimonials and success stories.
- **Week 6:** Create infographics on the ideation process and how Indie-Ideate helps.
- **Week 7:** Release an eBook on "10 Tips for Successful Ideation."
- **Week 8:** Publish a blog post on common pitfalls in software ideation.
- **Week 9:** Share behind-the-scenes content on product development.
- **Week 10:** Conduct a webinar on maximizing the use of Indie-Ideate.
- **Week 11:** Release a newsletter summarizing user feedback and updates.
- **Week 12:** Publish a final recap of the first 90 days and future plans.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30:** Finalize product features and pricing; begin pre-launch marketing.
- **D-0:** Official launch of Indie-Ideate; initiate all marketing channels.
- **D+30:** Collect user feedback; assess initial traction and adjust marketing strategies.
- **D+90:** Evaluate overall performance; set new targets based on user growth and revenue.

## Success Metrics
- **Daily Active Users (DAU):** Target 1,000 DAUs by D+90.
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue ($MRR):** Target $10,000 MRR by D+90.
