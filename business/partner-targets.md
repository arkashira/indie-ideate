# partner-targets.md

## Partner Integration Roadmap for Indie-Ideate

### Target Integrations

| Partner/API          | Free Tier Limits               | Integration Effort | Value-Add                                           | Rationale                                                                                   |
|----------------------|-------------------------------|--------------------|----------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Zapier**           | 5 Zaps, 100 Tasks/month       | M                  | Automate workflows for idea validation and feedback | Enables users to connect Indie-Ideate with other tools, enhancing productivity and reach.   |
| **Airtable**         | 1,200 records, 2GB attachment  | S                  | Organize and manage ideas and feedback              | Provides users with a structured way to track their ideation process, enhancing usability.   |
| **Stripe**           | No free tier, transaction fees | M                  | Monetization of validated ideas                      | Allows users to easily implement payment solutions for their tools, enhancing revenue potential. |
| **Notion**           | 1,000 blocks, 5 guests        | S                  | Documentation and collaboration on ideas            | Facilitates team collaboration and documentation, making it easier to refine ideas.         |
| **Google Cloud Functions** | 2 million invocations/month | L                  | Serverless execution for custom validation scripts   | Enables users to run custom scripts for idea validation, adding flexibility to the platform. |
| **Slack**            | 10 integrations, 5GB storage  | S                  | Real-time feedback and collaboration                 | Allows users to gather feedback quickly and collaborate in real-time, enhancing engagement.  |
| **Figma**            | 3 projects, unlimited viewers  | S                  | Visual prototyping of ideas                           | Helps users create visual representations of their ideas, aiding in the validation process.  |
| **Trello**           | 10 boards, 1 Power-Up/board   | S                  | Project management for idea development              | Provides a visual project management tool to track the progress of ideas, enhancing organization. |

### Prioritization Criteria
1. **Affiliate/Revenue-Share Potential**: Focus on partners that offer affiliate programs or revenue-sharing models to drive additional income for both Indie-Ideate and its users.
2. **Integration Effort**: Prioritize integrations that require a smaller effort to implement, allowing for quicker deployment and user feedback.
3. **Value-Add**: Ensure that each integration directly addresses user jobs related to ideation, validation, and monetization, enhancing the overall product offering.

### Next Steps
- Conduct outreach to selected partners to discuss integration possibilities and affiliate programs.
- Assess technical requirements and feasibility for the integrations listed above.
- Develop a timeline for integration implementation based on effort and potential impact.
