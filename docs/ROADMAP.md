# ROADMAP.md
## Introduction
The indie-ideate project aims to create an AI-powered ideation tool that assists indie hackers and creators in generating and validating software tool ideas. This roadmap outlines the key milestones and phases for the project, ensuring a clear and structured approach to delivering a high-quality product.

## MVP Milestone
The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) milestone is crucial for the initial launch of indie-ideate. The following features are considered MVP-critical:
### MVP-Critical Features
* **Idea Generation**: Implement a basic idea generation algorithm using natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning (ML) techniques.
* **Idea Validation**: Develop a simple validation mechanism that assesses the potential of generated ideas based on market demand and competition.
* **User Interface**: Design a user-friendly interface that allows users to input parameters, generate ideas, and view validation results.
* **Integration with Axentx Brain**: Establish a connection with the Axentx Brain (pgvector) to leverage the company's knowledge, memory, and datasets.

## v1 Phase
The v1 phase focuses on enhancing the MVP features and adding new functionalities to improve the overall user experience.
### v1 Themes
* **Idea Refinement**: Introduce advanced NLP and ML techniques to refine generated ideas and provide more accurate validation results.
* **Market Analysis**: Develop a market analysis module that provides users with insights into target audiences, competition, and potential revenue streams.
* **Community Features**: Implement community-driven features, such as idea sharing, commenting, and voting, to foster collaboration and feedback among users.
* **Axentx Brain Integration**: Enhance the integration with the Axentx Brain to leverage more datasets and improve the overall performance of the ideation tool.

## v2 Phase
The v2 phase concentrates on expanding the capabilities of indie-ideate and introducing new features that cater to the evolving needs of indie hackers and creators.
### v2 Themes
* **Advanced Validation**: Develop a more sophisticated validation mechanism that incorporates additional factors, such as technical feasibility, market trends, and user feedback.
* **Idea Prioritization**: Introduce an idea prioritization system that helps users focus on the most promising ideas based on their validation results and market potential.
* **Monetization Strategies**: Provide users with guidance on monetization strategies, including revenue models, pricing, and marketing techniques.
* **Integration with Other Axentx Products**: Explore opportunities to integrate indie-ideate with other Axentx products, such as vLLM and SGLang, to create a more comprehensive ecosystem for indie hackers and creators.

## Future Development
The indie-ideate roadmap will continue to evolve based on user feedback, market trends, and the growth of the Axentx ecosystem. Future development will focus on refining existing features, introducing new functionalities, and ensuring the product remains aligned with the needs of its target audience.
